Group 5: United by Interests, Defined by Differences

Our group shares a fascinating blend of common interests while maintaining unique individual pursuits. A notable common thread among us is our love for entertainment media and music. All three of us enjoy listening to music as a way to unwind and find peace. Mark and Marjun share a particular interest in anime and gaming, demonstrating their passion for interactive and animated entertainment. While Mildred's interests lean more towards physical activities like running and creative pursuits like journaling, she shares the group's appreciation for visual media through film watching. Despite our different approaches, we all use our hobbies as means of stress relief and finding joy in life.

  • Digital and Physical Balance: Our group combines both digital interests (gaming, technology) with physical or hands-on activities (running, gardening, journaling)
